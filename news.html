Event June 5, 2026

End-of-year International Education Showcase — Grades 4–6

On the last Friday of the school year, our Grade 4 to 6 students opened their classrooms to families for the annual International Education Showcase. Each class presented a bilingual project — from poster boards on global water access to short documentary films about Taoyuan's international transport links. The hall was packed, the English was impressive, and the pride on every student's face said it all.
